Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and major events, though we can sometimes accommodate shorter notice depending on availability.

What's included in your packages?

Our packages include professional shooting, basic editing, online gallery access, and high-resolution image delivery. Specific inclusions vary by package level.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We're available for destination shoots worldwide. Travel costs may apply depending on location and duration.

How long until I receive my photos/videos?

Edited photos are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks, while video productions may take 4-6 weeks depending on complexity.
